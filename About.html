About Us!

Midas Touch T-Shit Depot is a brand new online retail store brining our customers quality t-shirts at and affordable price. The company was founded by Group 7 in our Web Based Computing class and has been taking the retail industry by storm ever since. Our t-shirts are locally sourced, quality controlled and for each shirt purchased we donate 5% of the purchase price to Clothing The World; a charity that provides cloths for kinds in need around the world. This company was created with one simple goal, to cloth the world in t-shirts fine enough for King Midas himself to wear!
